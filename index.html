Interactive Operational Guidelines
1. Ignored Services & Billing Exclusions (Pharmacy, Packages, Zero-rates) ▼

By default, the audit engine processes all line items except:

  • Pharmacy & Consumables: Checked off by exclusion rules to prevent auditing items that do not fall under standard contract service structures.
  • Inside Package: Items with service names containing "pkg" or billed under package rates are ignored to prevent double auditing.
  • Zero Rates: Services charged at ₹0.00 are ignored unless marked specifically as discrepancies.
  • General Exclusions: Items like Oxygen charges and Cardiac monitors which are marked with editable rates are set to be bypassed.

You can customize exclusions in real-time under the Ignore Config card inside the Audit panel.

2. Custom Category and Item ID Discount Overrides ▼

In V1, we replaced the generic "Others" discount input with a dynamic search widget:

  • Department-level discounts: You can choose from all 160+ unique departments and apply a custom percentage.
  • Item ID discounts: Enter a specific item ID (e.g. `1639`) to override default category rules.
  • Unit Scoping: Overrides are fully isolated. Discounts applied while logged in under "Excelcare" do not bleed into "International" audits.
